**Worker: A**

## Group 4 — DFA and Waiver Wire
**Severity: Critical**
**Worker: A**

- [ ] GET /api/waivers returns array of current waiver wire players
- [ ] Each waiver entry has: player_id, player_name, position, overall, claim_window_games_remaining
- [ ] DFA'd player appears on waiver wire within same sim tick
- [ ] Waiver claim window = 3 games of sim time
- [ ] After 3 games unclaimed: player moves to free agent pool, disappears from /api/waivers
- [ ] Claimed player: removed from waiver wire, added to claiming team's 40-man roster
- [ ] Claim order follows reverse standings: worst team gets first claim
- [ ] Player with no options remaining who triggers send-down: DFA'd instead, generates news item
- [ ] Small market analytics GM teams have higher waiver claim rate than large market old-school teams (over 20-game sample)
