## CRITICAL FINDINGS

### AB-10 (Critical) — The live call-up/DFA/waiver chain is STILL end-to-end inert at runtime. AAA is now populated, but rank-based assignment makes AAA players strictly lower-rated than the 25-man, so every stat-based send-down/call-up trigger requires a same-position upgrade that can never exist. `call_up`, `dfa`, and `waiver_claim` = 0 in normal play on every seed across every season tested.

**Root cause (verified, two-part):**

**(a) AAA is populated but cannot, by construction, out-rate the 25-man at any position.** `assignRosterLevels` (`draft.ts:377-399`) sorts a team's drafted players `ORDER BY p.overall_rating DESC`, assigns ranks 0-24 to the 25-man (`is_on_25man=1`) and ranks 25-31 to AAA (`i < 32 ? 'AAA' : 'AA'`). So AAA is always ranks 26-32 — every AAA player's `overall_rating` is ≤ the lowest 25-man player's rating. But the triggers that would open and refill a slot all require a same-position AAA upgrade:
- Send-down Trigger 1 (`sendDown.ts:36-44`): `WHERE minor_level='AAA' AND position=?` then `aaaReplacement.overall_rating <= player.overall_rating → continue` (needs **strictly greater**).
- Send-down Trigger 2 (`sendDown.ts:64-70`): AAA SP at same position (any rating) — but gated behind `recent_starts >= 4` AND `recent ERA > 6.50`, which the recent-window reset (5-game cadence) makes hard to accumulate, and even when an AAA SP exists it is rank 26-32 caliber.
- Call-up Trigger 2 (`callup.ts:201-209`): needs AAA `>= mlbPlayer.overall_rating + 5`.
- Call-up Trigger 3 (`callup.ts:236-244`): needs AAA SP `>= sp.overall_rating` after recent ERA > 7.00.
- Call-up Trigger 4 (`callup.ts:262-270`): needs AAA `>= mlbStarter.overall_rating + 5`.

Call-up Trigger 1 (`callup.ts:130-185`, "roster < 25 → restore to full") does NOT require an upgrade — it fires on any roster hole. But **nothing ever opens a hole**: the send-down triggers (which would create the vacancy) are the upgrade-gated ones, and in-game injuries (`game.ts:758-769`) only truncate the box-score line via `applyInjuryTruncation` — they never set `is_on_25man=0` or remove a player from the active roster. So no 25-man slot ever opens, Trigger 1 never fires, and the cascade is dead at the send-down side.

**(b) The offseason promotion path works but cannot rescue reachability.** `runProspectPromotionStep` (`offseason.ts:163-206`) correctly promotes A→AA (rating≥50, age≤25) and AA→AAA (rating≥58, age≤27) — my 5-season probe shows 152/344/349/348 promotions per offseason. But promoted AAA players are still capped at the AA graduation tier and remain below the established 25-man regulars; even after 5 seasons of MLB aging-decline and prospect promotion, **same-position AAA upgrade pairs = 0 every season.** Prospect development (`prospectDev.ts`) explicitly keeps AAA "stable" per spec, so AAA ratings don't climb in-season either.

**File:line:** `draft.ts:384-399` (rank-based AAA = ranks 26-32, always below 25-man) + `sendDown.ts:44` / `callup.ts:209,244,270` (same-position higher-rating gate) + `game.ts:758-769` (injury never opens a 25-man slot).

**Evidence (Adversary organic in-engine probes, fresh worlds, NO manual seeding):**
- Minor-level distribution post-draft+spring (all seeds): `{AAA:100}` league-wide (= 20 teams × ~5 AAA each); `teamsWithAaa = 20/20`. **AAA population is genuinely fixed.**
- 25-man players with a higher-rated same-position AAA replacement (`upgradePairs`): **0 on seed 7, 11, 42**, and **0 in every one of 5 seasons** on seed 7.
- Full-season organic transaction counts: seed 7 `call_up:0 dfa:0 waiver_claim:0 send_down:3(spring)`; seed 11 `call_up:0 dfa:0 waiver_claim:0`; seed 42 `call_up:0 dfa:0 waiver_claim:0`. `maxRecentAb=20-22` (window accumulates — the Iter-3 ordering fix still works; the chain dies on the missing upgrade, not the window).
- 5-season seed 7 cumulative: `call_up 0,0,0,0,1` (the lone season-5 call-up is a `checkRosterInvariant` auto-promote repairing a team that came in at 24 after spring cuts — visible as "Team 4 (Condors): 24 → 25 on 25-man"); `dfa 0,0,0,0,0`; `waiver_claim 0,0,0,0,0`.

**Why the green suite missed it (again):** `callupSenddownReachability.test.ts` `beforeAll` (lines 49-66, 75-83) **manufactures** the prerequisites the real sim never produces — it re-levels an `A` player to AAA at the right position and boosts it `+10` (line 59), OR boosts an existing AAA player to `on25Man.overall_rating + 10` (line 65), AND sets `recent_ab=25, recent_hits=0` (line 76). The DFA sub-test (lines 184-191) does the same plus `options_remaining=0`. So the test proves "given a manufactured AAA upgrade and a manufactured low-OPS window, the loop fires a send-down/DFA" — true, but the real sim has neither. `iter4Regression.test.ts` runs 45+20 organic games but its §5.1 assertions check only AAA *population* (`aaaCount >= 1`, `leagueAaaCount >= teams×4`, `maxAaaRating >= 40`) and a relaxed `maxAaa >= minOn25` overall-rating check (line 142) — it **never asserts a single in-season `call_up`/`dfa`/`waiver_claim` transaction**, which is precisely the assertion the Architect's eval-3 §4 mandated ("assert in-season call-up/send-down/DFA/waiver counts ≥1 each").

**Impact:** Fails Test Group 4 (call-ups) and Group 5 (send-downs/DFA/waivers) wholesale at runtime — identical user-visible outcome to the Iter-1/2/3 Criticals. ROSTER-badge in-season news (call-ups, in-season send-downs, DFA), TRANSACTION-badge waiver-claim news, and `/api/waivers` content are all empty in normal play. The prompt is explicit: "If the call-up/DFA/waiver chain is still not end-to-end connected, this is still Critical." It is not connected. **Critical, UNRESOLVED.**

**Resolution required:** Make a same-position AAA upgrade reachable, or make a roster vacancy reachable independent of upgrades. Concretely, any one of: (1) persist in-game injuries as a temporary `is_on_25man=0` IL stint so call-up Trigger 1 fires (the cleanest fix — injuries already occur ~0.4/game now); (2) relax the send-down OPS/ERA triggers to not require a *higher-rated* AAA replacement (a struggling regular can be optioned for an equal-or-lesser AAA arm/bat, which is realistic); or (3) seed a few AAA players at or above some 25-man fringe ratings at worldgen/promotion (e.g., put high-ceiling prospects above the weakest bench player). Then add a no-manual-seeding full-season `runGameTick` integration test that asserts in-season `call_up`/`dfa`/`waiver_claim` counts each ≥1 — the assertion the suite has lacked for four iterations.
