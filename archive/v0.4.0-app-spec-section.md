## Scope
v0.4.0 is the depth release. Everything that makes a dynasty feel like a living world over 10+ seasons. No new visual zones — this release deepens what's already there. Every feature below goes in.

## Simultaneous Event Priority Order
When multiple rare events fire on the same tick, resolve in this order:
1. **Tragedy** — pauses sim immediately, all other events deferred to next tick
2. **Gambling ban** — removes player, processes before any transactions
3. **PED third offense** — removes player, processed before waiver claims
4. **Relocation threat** — queued, resolves at season end only
5. **Injury / suspension** — normal processing
6. **Minor league cascading** — last, after all roster changes settle

## 2. Financial Depth

### Enhanced Financials Tab (team detail)
**Year-over-year panel:**
- Revenue trend: bar chart, one bar per season
- Attendance trend: avg attendance per game per season
- Payroll history: actual payroll spent vs budget per season
- Luxury tax paid: cumulative and per-season
- Owner investment tracking: budget changes year-over-year

**Revenue model (expanded):**
```
annual_revenue = base_market_revenue
  + (winning_pct - 0.500) × performance_bonus
  + championship_bonus
  + playoff_appearance_bonus
  - losing_streak_penalty
  × owner_investment_modifier
```

**Payroll budget update logic (offseason):**
- If revenue > budget × 1.3: owner increases budget 5-15% (analytics GM lobbies harder)
- If revenue < budget × 0.8 for 2 consecutive seasons: budget decreases 5-10%
- Mega market owners: budget floor never below $120M
- Small market hands-off owner: least likely to invest windfall back into payroll

**Performance requirement:** Response time < 300ms for 10 seasons, < 600ms for 50+ seasons.

## 3. Hall of Fame

### Career Thresholds
Hitters (any one qualifies):
- 400 career HR, OR
- 3000 career hits, OR
- Career OPS+ > 130 over 10+ seasons, OR
- 8+ seasons with OPS > .900

Pitchers (any one qualifies):
- 250 career wins, OR
- 3000 career strikeouts, OR
- Career ERA < 3.00 over 10+ seasons, OR
- 5+ seasons with ERA < 2.50

### Eligibility
- 1-year waiting period after retirement before appearing on ballot
- Maximum 10 years on ballot — removed if not inducted by year 10
- PED flag displayed with `[PED]` marker in vote share UI — analytics voters penalize heavily, old-school voters split

### Voting (procedural, each offseason)
- 30 voters (one per franchise), personality: old-school voters favor counting stats, analytics voters favor efficiency metrics
- 75% vote threshold to induct
- Max 3 players inducted per year
- Vote share stored and displayed per player per season on ballot

### Veterans Committee
- Runs every 5 seasons
- Reviews: ballot washouts (10 years, never inducted), tragedy victims (auto-consideration), managers/GMs with exceptional careers
- Can induct 1 player per 5-season cycle
- Tragedy victim HOF induction shown as "Special Induction — Veterans Committee"

### Hall of Fame Tab (new top-level tab)
- Inductee browser: filter by position, franchise, era, induction season
- Each inductee card: career stats, franchise(s), induction year, vote share history
- Tragedy inductees get memorial marker
- Manager/GM wing: separate section

### API
- `GET /api/halloffame` — all inductees
- `GET /api/halloffame/:playerId` — single inductee full card

## 4. Player-to-Coach Pipeline

### Retirement → Coaching Pool
Retired player with leadership >= 70 AND coachability >= 65:
- Enters `coaching_candidates` table
- Specialty by position: C/SP → pitching_coach, SS/2B → bench_coach or manager candidate, OF (speed) → third_base_coach, 1B/DH → hitting_coach
- Coaching rating = leadership × 0.5 + coachability × 0.3 + career_overall × 0.2
- Former stars (career overall 80+): +10 to coaching rating

### Hiring
- Available league-wide
- Old-school GMs favor high career stats; analytics GMs favor high coachability
- Franchise legend (10+ seasons): priority consideration for manager openings at that team, "returning hero" news item generated
- Player encyclopedia shows full arc: playing career → coaching career

### Career Arc Continuity
- Player-turned-manager who wins championship: Timeline newspaper callout
- Full arc tracked in franchise history

### New DB table: coaching_candidates
- id, player_id, specialty, coaching_rating, available (bool), hired_team_id, hired_season, available_since

## 5. Live Minor League Movement (Performance-Based Cascading)

### Performance Evaluation (every 5 games)

**Promotion triggers (2 consecutive evaluations required):**
```
AA  → AAA:    AVG > .310 over 20+ AB, or ERA < 2.40 over 15+ IP
A   → AA:     AVG > .320 over 20+ AB, or ERA < 2.20 over 15+ IP
Rookie → A:   AVG > .330 over 15+ AB, or ERA < 2.00 over 10+ IP
```

**Demotion triggers (2 consecutive evaluations required):**
```
AAA → AA:   AVG < .200 over 25+ AB, or ERA > 6.50 over 20+ IP
AA  → A:    AVG < .190 over 25+ AB, or ERA > 7.00 over 20+ IP
```

**Cascading:** When a promotion opens a spot, the best eligible player at the level below gets evaluated same tick. A prospect can cascade A → AA → AAA in one season. Each level move generates a news item.

**Logjam:** If AAA player meets promotion criteria but MLB incumbent hasn't hit demotion criteria:
- Analytics GM: demotes incumbent after 1 failed eval window
- Old-school GM: waits for 2 failed eval windows
- "Logjam" news item generated

**Empty level:** If cascading empties a level entirely, procedurally generate 2-3 replacement-level players (overall 35-45) same tick.

### Minor League Standings
Four levels, updated every 5 games. W/L/PCT/GB. Game outcomes weighted by aggregate affiliate talent. No box scores.

### Prospect Rankings
Updated every 10 games. Ranked by: potential × 0.4 + current_overall × 0.3 + recent_performance × 0.3.
- Per-team top 10 shown in Minors tab
- League-wide top 50 in Players tab ("Top Prospects" toggle)

### Performance requirement
Cascading evaluation for all 20 franchises must complete within a single setImmediate tick.

### API
- `GET /api/minors/standings` — all 4 levels
- `GET /api/players/prospects` — league-wide top 50

## 6. Injuries with Rehab Assignments

### Severity Tiers
| Tier | Duration | Rehab Games |
|---|---|---|
| day_to_day | 1-3 games | 0 |
| short_il | 7-15 games | 3 |
| standard_il | 20-45 games | 5 |
| long_il | 60-90 games | 8 |
| season_ending | Rest of season | 0 (returns next spring) |

### Injury Types (stored in injury_type field)
- **arm** (pitchers): velocity -2-5 on return, 10% chance permanent
- **hamstring**: speed -1-2 temporarily, full recovery likely
- **oblique**: power -3-5 for 10 games post-return
- **concussion**: composure drops on return, longer recovery
- **tommy_john** (pitchers): season-ending, velocity -3-5 on return, may recover over 2 seasons

### Rehab Flow
1. Player on IL → `is_injured = true`, `injury_return_game` set
2. After (duration - rehab_games) games: assigned to AAA rehab (or AA if no slot)
3. Rehab games sim at minor league level (health check only, no stats)
4. After rehab complete: activated to 25-man
5. Reaggravation: 15% chance during rehab → IL extended 50%, timer resets

### Medical Staff Rating (1-10, generated at world gen, upgradeable in offseason)
- 8-10: duration -15%, reaggravation risk halved
- 4-7: baseline
- 1-3: duration +10%, reaggravation risk +5%

### Pitcher Innings Limits
- Limit = `stamina × 2` innings per season
- Analytics GM: removes SP from rotation at 90% of limit, skips start at 100%
- Old-school GM: ignores until ERA spike over 3 consecutive starts
- Exceeding limit: 20% arm injury risk next season, velocity -1 permanent

### New DB fields
- players: injury_type (VARCHAR CHECK IN arm|hamstring|oblique|concussion|tommy_john), injury_tier (VARCHAR CHECK IN day_to_day|short_il|standard_il|long_il|season_ending), rehab_games_remaining (INTEGER DEFAULT 0, CHECK >= 0), career_injuries (INTEGER DEFAULT 0)
- teams: medical_staff_rating (INTEGER DEFAULT 5, CHECK 1-10)

## 7. Tragedies

### Probability Model
```
Player:   0.01% base
  age 18-22: ×1.5  age 23-32: ×1.0  age 33-37: ×1.2  age 38+: ×1.8
  MLB roster: ×1.3   Rookie/A: ×0.7

Manager:  0.025% base
  age < 50: ×0.7   age 50-65: ×1.0   age 65+: ×2.0

Owner:    0.5% per season (unchanged — handles existing death event)
```
Never fires during an active game. Always between game ticks.

### Event Flow
1. Sim pauses immediately (per priority order — takes precedence over turbo)
2. LLM generates obituary — single dedicated call, never batched, 30s timeout max
3. News item pinned at top of feed for 5 games
4. All 20 teams: memorial patch for remainder of season
5. Player number retired by their franchise
6. 3-game league pause (3 game ticks skipped for all teams)
7. Morale effects:
   - Player death: win probability -3% for 10 games
   - Manager death: win probability +2% "play for him" for 15 games; bench coach interim
8. HOF veterans committee auto-consideration at next 5-season cycle
9. Timeline above-the-fold treatment that season
10. Encyclopedia: memorial marker, potential shown as "unrealized" for players

### LLM Tone Rules
- Cause of death always vague: "passed away unexpectedly", "died in a tragic accident", "succumbed to a sudden illness"
- No graphic details, no sensationalism
- Prompt instructs: "Write a respectful, mournful tribute. Focus on the person, their career, and what they meant to their team and the league."
- LLM timeout must not block sim — if call fails, insert procedural fallback obituary string

### New DB fields
- players: memorial (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false), retired_number (INTEGER nullable CHECK 0-99)
- leagues: memorial_patch_season (INTEGER nullable)

## 8. Suspensions

### On-Field Suspensions (procedural)
- **Brawl:** triggers when a team has been HBP 3+ times in last 5 games and another HBP occurs, or a player with aggression flag is involved. Multiple players suspended 3-5 games, manager 5-7 games.
- **Dirty play:** flagrant slide or intentional head-level pitch. 5-10 game suspension.

### Off-Field Suspensions (probabilistic)
- **PED:** 0.2% per player per season, +50% weight for age 33+ with declining overall
  - First offense: 50-game suspension
  - Second offense: 100-game suspension
  - Third offense: lifetime ban (processed per priority order — immediate removal, no waiver pass, memorial page preserved with [GAMBLING BAN]-style overlay)
- **Conduct violation:** 10-30 games
- **Gambling:** 0.05% per player per season, lifetime ban, nuclear news event (gravity matches tragedy)

### Suspension Effects
- Suspended player occupies roster spot — cannot be replaced by call-up
- PED return: leadership -10, coachability -5 permanently; HOF ballot shows [PED] marker
- Gambling ban: player permanently removed, `gambling_ban = true`, HOF ineligible permanently, encyclopedia page preserved with `[GAMBLING BAN]` header overlay
- Brawl: team morale +5 short-term, -2 long-term
- Transaction types added: `suspended`, `reinstated`

### New DB fields
- players: suspension_games_remaining (INTEGER DEFAULT 0 CHECK 0-200), suspension_type (VARCHAR nullable CHECK IN brawl|dirty_play|ped|conduct|gambling), ped_offenses (INTEGER DEFAULT 0 CHECK 0-3), gambling_ban (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false)

## 9. Player Personality Depth

### Clubhouse Chemistry
`chemistry_score` (0-100) on teams, server-calculated only — never submitted by client.
Recalculated every 10 games:
```
chemistry_score =
  avg(leadership, 25-man roster) × 0.4
  + avg(coachability, 25-man roster) × 0.3
  + win_streak_modifier × 0.2
  + veteran_core_bonus × 0.1   # 3+ players with 5+ seasons together

Win probability effect:
  > 75: +2%    50-75: 0%    25-49: -1%    < 25: -3%
```

**Malcontent:** leadership < 30 AND overall < 75:
- 5% chance per season of `is_malcontent = true`
- Chemistry -15 while on roster
- News item generated
- GM confidence -5 if not moved within 10 games
- Analytics GM trades within 10 games; old-school GM slower

**"Address the Clubhouse" directive:** If owned franchise chemistry < 25, a new suggested nudge appears in the owner directives panel (one-time use, no cooldown cost). Resolves trade demand faster.

### Trade Demands
Star (overall 80+) with leadership < 40, on team with chemistry < 30, 10+ games under .500:
- 15% chance per season of `trade_demand_active = true`
- News item generated
- If not traded within 15 games: all ratings -3 for rest of season
- If traded: ratings restored at new team

### Loyalty Discounts
Player with leadership >= 75 and 5+ seasons with same team:
- In free agency: accepts 10-15% below market to stay
- `loyalty_discount_eligible = true`
- News item generated on signing

### Work Ethic and Aging
- work_ethic >= 75: decline starts age 34 (not 33), rate halved
- work_ethic <= 35: decline starts age 31, rate +50%
- work_ethic >= 80: 20% chance per season of "fountain of youth" at age 36-38 (ratings tick up 1-2)

### Coachability and Development
- coachability >= 80 + coaching_staff >= 7: extra development tick per 10 games
- coachability <= 30: development stalls entirely, regardless of coaching staff
- coachability >= 75 AND potential = B: 10% chance per season of late bloomer upgrade to A

### New DB fields
- teams: chemistry_score (INTEGER DEFAULT 50 CHECK 0-100)
- players: is_malcontent (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false), trade_demand_active (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false), loyalty_discount_eligible (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false)

## 10. Team Sales Depth + Relocation

### Sale Types
| Type | Trigger | Fan Reaction Tone |
|---|---|---|
| distressed | Revenue < budget × 0.7 for 2 seasons | Relief, cautious optimism |
| succession | Owner death heir declines | Mourning + uncertainty |
| investment | Post-championship cash-out | Outrage at beloved owner selling |
| hostile | Outside investor, no local ties | Fear of relocation |

### Franchise Valuation
```
franchise_value (stored in millions) =
  base_market_value
  + (total_wins × 0.1M)
  + (championships × 50M)
  + (seasons_in_league × 2M)
  + attendance_premium (mega: +100M, large: +40M, medium: +10M, small: +0)
```
Sale price = franchise_value × sale_type_modifier (distressed: 0.7×, investment: 1.3×, hostile: 1.5×, succession: 1.0×).
News item and timeline record include sale price.

### Relocation Threat
Triggers when ALL conditions met:
- New owner (sale happened this season or last)
- franchise_value < league median
- stadium_capacity < 30,000
- attendance < 60% capacity for 2 consecutive seasons

Flow:
1. News item: "New [Team] ownership threatens to relocate franchise"
2. `relocation_threat_active = true`
3. 10-game negotiation window (per priority order: resolves at season end only)
4. City council vote (procedural): small market 40% save, medium market 65%
5. If saved: stadium_deal, capacity +, revenue boost, threat resolved
6. If not saved: relocation fires at season end
   - New city + nickname + abbreviation generated
   - Same roster, same front office
   - `original_city` field populated
   - Franchise history carries over with "[Old City] era" labels
   - LLM relocation narrative (batched with offseason calls)
   - Timeline landmark entry
   - Owned franchise: Watch tab skyline regenerates for new market size, all directives carry over

### New DB fields
- teams: franchise_value (INTEGER CHECK >= 0), stadium_deal_active (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false), relocation_threat_active (BOOLEAN DEFAULT false), original_city (VARCHAR nullable)

## Known Edge Cases

- **Tragedy during turbo:** sim pauses immediately per priority order, takes precedence over burst completion
- **Player on IL when tragedy fires:** tragedy supersedes IL status, memorial fires normally
- **Relocation mid-season:** threat flags set but resolution deferred to season end per priority order
- **Gambling ban for owned franchise player:** sim pauses, owner directive panel disabled temporarily, LLM generates special narrative
- **HOF ballot player dies before induction:** auto-inducted via veterans committee next cycle, vote share shown as "Special Induction"
- **Chemistry < 25 for owned franchise:** "Address the Clubhouse" directive suggestion appears in nudge panel
- **Relocation of owned franchise:** Watch tab skyline regenerates for new market size, all directives and GM confidence carry over
- **PED third offense:** removed immediately per priority order, no waiver pass, encyclopedia preserved with overlay marker
- **Minor league level emptied by cascading:** generate 2-3 replacement-level players (overall 35-45) same tick
- **LLM tragedy call timeout:** if call fails after 30s, insert procedural fallback obituary string — sim never blocked
- **Rehab player's team gets relocated:** rehab assignment follows player, no interruption to rehab timeline
